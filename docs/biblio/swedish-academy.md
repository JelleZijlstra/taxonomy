# _Kungl. Svenska vetenskapsakademiens handlingar_

The name of the acts of the Swedish Academy of Science changed slightly over time. I
triangulated the names from
[the BHL bibliography](https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/bibliography/50688):

- ser. 1
  - _Kongl. Svenska Vetenskaps Academiens Handlingar_ vol. 1–17 (1739–1756)
  - _Kongl. Vetenskaps Academiens Handlingar_ vol. 18–40 (1757–1779)
- ser. 2
  - _Kongl. Vetenskaps Academiens Nya Handlingar_ vol. 1–33 (1780–1812)
- ser. 3
  - _Kongl. Vetenskaps-Academiens Handlingar_ vol. 1–42 (1813–1854)
- N.F.
  - _Kongliga Svenska Vetenskaps-Akademiens Handlingar_ vol. 1–35 (1855–1901)
  - _Kungliga Svenska Vetenskaps-Akademiens Handlingar_ vol. 36–38 (1902–1904)
  - _Kungliga Svenska Vetenskapsakademiens Handlingar_ vol. 39–63 (1904–)
